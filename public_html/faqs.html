Why do ghosts linger on the campus?

Ghosts linger because their spirits are in a state of limbo- unable to pass onto the afterlife due to great emotional trauma or due to unfinished ties that they still have on earth.

Why are some ghosts good and why are some bad?

Just like with people, ghosts just behave differently and they have their own unique and distince personalities. Tips

Tips


Avoid Walking Alone!

If possible, avoid walking alone at night. A lot of paranormal activity occur when individuals are alone and it is dark out.

Do Not Annoy Ghosts

Annoying ghosts is a dangerous activity, we haven't fully understood their powers and motives, and of course, some ghosts are more malicious than others. Since ghosts are different from each other, it is imperative that we do not annoy them, as to not invoke their (possible) ire.
